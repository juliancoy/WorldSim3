# Layer Expansion Plan

## Must-have
- Parcel
- Real Property Information
- Zoning
- Housing and Building Permits
- Vacant Building Notices
- NIBRS Group A Crime Data (2022-present)

## Nice-to-have
- Vacant Building Rehabs
- Tax Lien Certificate Sale Properties
- Open Bid List - Vacants to Value
- Completed City Demo
- Major Drainage
- Water Infrastructure
- Rate of Clogged Storm Drain Reports (CSA)
- Median Price of Homes Sold (CSA)
- Total Number of Businesses (CSA)

## Heavy-data
- 2021 Tax Sale List
- Part 1 Crime Data (Legacy SRS)

## Next desirable additions (high value)
- Building footprints and heights/floors
- Transit GTFS (routes, stops, frequencies)
- Road speed limits and one-way restrictions
- Bike lanes and trail network
- FEMA flood zones and sea-level risk
- Tree canopy / heat vulnerability
- 311 service requests and code violations
- School catchments and public facilities
- Hospital/clinic and food access
- Census tract/block-group ACS demographics and income
